Lara's Yummy Burrito

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Salsa
  • Greek Yog or Cottage Cheese
  • Cheese
  • Spinach
  • Tortilla Wrap

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients and lay out Tortilla Wrap
  2. Smooth Yogurt onto torilla as base layer
  3. Add beef, cheese, spinach, and salsa in desired amounts
  4. (Optional) Sear wrap on hot pan to enhance flavour and make easier to hold
