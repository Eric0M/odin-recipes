Lemonade Frosty

Return to main page

Description

This sweet and tangy lemonade milkshake is as refreshing as it is decadent, and easy to mix up for a group.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 12 ounces frozen lemonade concentrate
  • 12 ounces frozen lemonade concentrate

Steps

  1. Place ice cream, frozen lemonade concentrate, and lemon zest in the jar of a blender, and blend until smooth. Divide into 4 glasses, and serve immediately.
